At Radian:
Innovating sustainable water solutions for a cleaner, healthier future.

At Radian, we possess extensive experience in the design of water and wastewater networks, as well as wastewater and industrial treatment plants. Our designs include fully integrated containerized plants and advanced treatment systems for reusing high-quality water in irrigation and industrial applications. We use the latest hydraulic software and global modeling tools to ensure accuracy and efficiency in all our projects.

Our specialized engineering and technical teams oversee major projects for both public and private sectors in diverse fields, including treatment plants, water networks, and wastewater and industrial drainage systems. Our services include:

  • Water distribution networks: Designing efficient systems to ensure reliable water supply.
  • Wastewater networks: Developing integrated solutions for flow control and wastewater treatment.
  • Firefighting networks: Implementing robust systems that enhance facility readiness and safety.
  • Irrigation networks: Creating sustainable irrigation systems to support ideal agricultural growth.
  • Desalination and groundwater treatment plants: Providing advanced solutions to address water scarcity challenges.
  • Wastewater and industrial treatment plants: Designing efficient systems for water treatment and reuse.
